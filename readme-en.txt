# TaskPortKiller

TaskPortKiller is a lightweight and powerful tool that allows you to easily kill tasks and free up ports on your system.

## Features
- List and kill running processes.
- List and kill processes that occupy specific ports.
- Dynamic process and port scanning.
- Small size, portable, no installation needed.
- Admin rights are automatically requested when necessary.

## How to Use
1. Run TaskPortKiller.exe as Administrator (admin rights are requested automatically).
2. Choose the operation:
   - Kill a process by its PID.
   - Kill a port by its number.
   - List running processes and their PIDs.
   - List open ports and their PIDs.
3. Follow on-screen instructions.

## Requirements
- Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
- PowerShell 5.0 or higher
- Administrator privileges
